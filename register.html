Africa Business Innovation Forum 2023

September 14th
La Palm Royal Beach Hotel

Africa Business Innovation Forum is uniquely positioned to drive innovation and growth among SMEs and startups in Ghana – and across Africa. By leveraging a broad network of business executives from MNCs, policy makers and academics, ABIForum has been able to deliver value to SME owners and startup founders by bringing new perspectives, equipping them with practical frameworks and challenges to pursue paths that they never thought possible.

The goal of Africa Business innovation Forum is to adopt a multi-stakeholder solution to creating a robust SME sector and improving solutions on technology, healthcare, poverty reduction, food and energy security in Africa.
