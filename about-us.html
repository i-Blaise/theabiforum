About Us

Who we are

Here at Africa Business Innovation Forum (ABIForum), who we are begins with three questions:
• How can we place innovative SMEs and startups in Africa on a higher pedestal of growth and innovation?
• How can we improve global visibility and reach of African SMEs and startups?
• How can we engage the capital, intellect and network of multiple stakeholders to address key challenges of startup and SME development in Africa?
‍
We have a two-path approach to answering these questions and realizing their inherent goals:
• By convening relevant stakeholders across the public and private sectors to have policy-changing and paradigm shifting conversations around SME development.
• Through the ABIForum Network: a network of business executives, founders, policy makers and relevant stakeholders for constant engagement and collaborations.

Our Mission

Africa Business Innovation Forum aims to build a leading platform for stakeholder engagement towards private-public partnerships, research and policy development to foster the growth of innovative businesses in Africa.

Our Goal

The goal of Africa Business innovation Forum is to adopt a multi-stakeholder solution to creating a robust SME sector and improving solutions on technology, healthcare, poverty reduction, food and energy security in Africa.
