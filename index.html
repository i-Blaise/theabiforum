It’s more than just a forum!

Africa Business Innovation Forum is uniquely positioned to drive innovation and growth among SMEs and startups in Ghana – and across Africa. By leveraging a broad network of business executives from MNCs, policy makers and academics, ABIForum has been able to deliver value to SME owners and startup founders by bringing new perspectives, equipping them with practical frameworks and challenges to pursue paths that they never thought possible.

What do you gain from attending the ABIForum?

Through our affiliate advisors and consultants, the Forum provides a set of post-events for SME owners and founders.

Growth Masterclass

Participating businesses get a rare opportunity to attend a Masterclass organized by an affiliate international executive business school with world renowned instructors.

Executive Ball

Launching this year, the ABIForum Executive Ball will give participating businesses and individuals a chance to interact with keynote speakers, panelists, policy makers and business executives in an intimate setting.

Abiforum Network

Opportunities to network among participating businesses and stakeholders do not stop after the event. Participants have a chance for exclusive membership into the ABIForum network for continuous engagement.
